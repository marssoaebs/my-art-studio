About Me

An Artist Passionate About Teaching Young Minds

A Software Engineer by profession and an artist by passion — I specialise in guiding children aged 4–10 through the joy of painting, combining a logical mind with a creative heart.

Every child is a natural artist. My role is to give them the tools, techniques, and the confidence to express themselves freely. Classes are kept small so every child gets individual attention.

🖌️ Watercolour 🎭 Acrylic Painting ✏️ Sketching 🌿 Nature Art 🎉 Mixed Media
